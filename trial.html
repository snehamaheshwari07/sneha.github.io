About Me

Hello! I'm Sneha Maheshwari, a passionate professional in computer science and applied mathematics. My journey began in 11th grade, aiming for the challenging JEE Advance. At the age of 14, I committed myself to rigorous study, eventually securing rank 3141 among 2 million candidates. This led me to an Integrated Masters in Applied Mathematics at IIT Roorkee, where I immersed myself in projects and internships, culminating in a software developer role at Amagi.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta. My work experience includes software development at Amagi and data analysis at American Express, with a strong foundation in languages like C++, Python, and SQL, and tools like Jupyter and AWS.

I'm driven by a desire to blend theoretical knowledge with practical applications, particularly in machine learning and data analytics. Outside work, I find joy in reading, sketching, and painting.
